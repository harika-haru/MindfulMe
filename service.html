When to See a Therapist

Take this questionnaire to assess your mental health


Have you been experiencing persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, or low mood?

Have you noticed a significant change in your sleep patterns (too much or too little) over the past few weeks?

Are you finding it difficult to concentrate or make decisions in your daily life?

Have you lost interest or pleasure in activities that you used to enjoy?

Do you frequently experience overwhelming anxiety or panic attacks?

Have you been avoiding social interactions or isolating yourself from others?

Have you noticed a change in your appetite (significant weight loss or gain) without trying to do so?

Are you struggling with intrusive or obsessive thoughts that are causing distress?

Have you experienced a traumatic event that continues to affect you emotionally?

Do you have difficulty managing anger or controlling your temper?

Are you engaging in self-destructive behaviors, such as self-harm or substance abuse?

Have you been experiencing relationship difficulties that are causing significant distress?

Do you have thoughts of hurting yourself or others?

Have you been feeling overwhelmed and unable to cope with life's challenges?

Have others expressed concern about your mental health or well-being?
